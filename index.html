About Quinten Goens

A professional backstory on a particular data analyst and engineer active in the Aviation Industry working on trajectory based operational performance monitoring.

Education

Bachelor of Science: Physics and Astronomy

A Bachelors of Physics program at the Vrije Universiteit Brussels in which I was exposed to a broad variety of theoretical and experimental physics through lectures and lab sessions, supplemented with thorough introductions to other scientific disciplines and data analysis. My thesis on the topic of the Determination of Intergalactic Dust Properties through High Energy Emission from Active Galactic Nuclei was conducted at the Interuniversity Institute for High Energies (IIHE).

Master of Science: Physics and Astronomy

With a minor in Economy & Business

A Master's degree in Physics and Astronomy and a Minor in Economy and Business at the Vrije Universiteit Brussels. The coursework included lecture series on a variety of physics subjects. My thesis was conducted at the Interuniversity Institute for High Energies (IIHE) in cooperation with the IceCube Collaboration. Topic of my thesis: "Investigation of the Precusor Phase of Gamma-Ray Bursts".

Professional Experience

Data Engineer

At Cartel Damage Claims SRL (From 2019 until 2022)

Working as a data engineer in the multidisciplinary and multinational data analysis team for antitrust damage litigations at Cartel Damage Claims. We deploy, maintain and use data analysis and data warehousing systems to support our ETL procedures.

  • About Cartel Damage Claims SRL

Performance Data Analyst

At EUROCONTROL, Aviation Intelligence Unit & Performance Review Unit (Started in 2022)

Working as a data analyst and engineer in the multinational data analysis team for Operational Performance Review Services (OPS) at EUROCONTROL. In addition, as a data analyst, supporting work is carried out for the Performance Review Commission (PRC) and European Commission (EC) Single European Sky Performance Scheme (SES-PS) as part of the Performance Review Unit (PRU).
